Inspired by the movie “2001: Space Odyssey”, more specifically, the link between the prehistoric phase and the idea of human evolution and futurism. That said, the theme above all portrays a relationship between ideas, concepts and techniques that, on the one hand, are natural, raw, spontaneous and refer to an idealization of prehistoric values and ancestors, and, on the other hand, are sophisticated, delicate, minimalist and portray futuristic and, in a way, modernist concepts. This relationship will then create a new universe within the book where these ideas and techniques can coexist.
