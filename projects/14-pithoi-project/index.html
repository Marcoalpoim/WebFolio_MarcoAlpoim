The concept of the graphic brand Píthoi is intended to represent an "open-source" self-irrigating pot for plants. A sustainable, ergonomic and automatic (micro:bit) pot, thought and designed to reuse rainwater. The graphic brand is composed of the symbol, which represents the vase, the name, Píthoi, and the brand's signature, self-watering vase. Regarding the language of the visual identity, it is Inspired, especially the aesthetics, by minimalist, simple and modern forms and characteristics.
