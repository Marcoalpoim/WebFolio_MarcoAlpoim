Hi, I'm Marco Alpoim — a multidisciplinary designer with a background in Communication Design and Visual Arts. Over the past five years, I've specialized in Digital Design, Web Development, UX/UI, User-Centered Design, Branding, and Editorial Design, often within cultural and creative contexts. My work is guided by a minimalist and pragmatic approach, allowing me to navigate seamlessly between digital experiences and visual storytelling.

I'M CURRENTLY WORKING IN THE DIGITAL MARKETING/ E-COMMERCE AREA AT TELEPIZZA PORTUGAL, AS A MULTIDISCIPLINARY GRAPHIC DESIGNER/UX UI FRONTEND DEVELOPER.
